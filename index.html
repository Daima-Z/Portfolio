About Me

Hello and welcome to my portfolio! My name is Daima Zainab, and I am a textile and graphic designer based in Ireland. Originally from Pakistan, I have always been fascinated by the intricate designs and patterns of traditional textiles, which led me to pursue a degree in Textile Design BS Honors. Since then, I have honed my skills in designing fabrics, prints, and patterns that are both aesthetically pleasing and functional. I love experimenting with different techniques and materials to create unique and inspiring designs that tell a story and evoke emotions. Recently, I completed a graphic design course, which has broadened my skillset and allowed me to offer a wider range of design services. I have designed logos, visiting cards, and letterheads for several companies, and I enjoy the challenge of creating designs that capture the essence of a brand and its values. I am a creative individual who is always eager to learn and take on new challenges. Whether it's designing textiles or graphics, I strive to bring my best to every project and exceed my clients' expectations. Thank you for taking the time to visit my portfolio, and I look forward to the opportunity to work with you!
